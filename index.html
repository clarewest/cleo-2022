How to Get Here

We recommend travelling by public transport or taxi. If you have any questions about getting here please feel free to get in touch with us.

  • Transport

Transport

The Orangery is a 10 minute walk from Holland Park (Central line) or High Street Kensington (Circle and District line).

From Holland Park to the Lighterman is around 30 minutes by public transport. Take the Central line (Eastbound) and Victoria line (Northbound) from Holland Park to King's Cross St Pancras, changing at Oxford Circus.

The Lighterman is a few minutes’ walk from King’s Cross and St Pancras stations. Follow signs for Regent’s Canal, cross the bridge over the canal and the Lighterman is on the right of Granary Square.

Alternatively, transport by Uber takes around 30 minutes (approximately £17), depending on traffic.

The Neighbourhoods

Places to explore around the venues

Holland Park

A public park in Kensington, the former grounds of Cope Castle built in 1605

Kyoto Garden

A Japanese garden within Holland Park, gifted by the city of Kyoto in 1991

Coal Drops Yard

Formerly a derelict Victorian industrial yard, now a regenerated public space and shopping complex

Granary Square

Canalside public leisure space in London's 'Knowledge Quarter', where we both work

Questions

  • What time should I arrive?

    The venue is open to guests from 1.30pm, and the ceremony will begin at 2.00pm, please arrive at least 15 minutes early.

  • What happens at the ceremony venue?

    After a short ceremony, there will be a drinks reception with sparkling wine, tea, coffee, and light canapes on the lawn outside the Orangery (weather permitting).

  • What happens between the ceremony and the reception?

    The drinks reception at the Orangery will end at 4.30pm, and the reception begins at the Lighterman at 6pm. Travel time between the venues is around 40 minutes by tube or taxi.

    We recommend spending some time exploring the gardens at Holland Park and the Kyoto Garden (there is also a cafe), or heading to Granary Square early to explore the shops, bars, Canopy Market or the canalside.

  • What happens at the restaurant?

    We will have private access to the first floor dining room at the Lighterman, including the terrace. The ground floor will remain open to the public.

  • Where can I park?

    We recommend arriving by public transport or taxi, as this is the easiest way to get around in London.

    Pay and display parking is available at Abbotsbury Road Car Park. The charge is £3.70 per hour on Sundays from 07:30–18:30

    The nearest car park to the Lighterman is the multi-storey Handyside Car Park which is open until 22:00. The evening rate (entry after 17:00) is £8.

  • What's for dinner?

    Dinner will be provided at the Lighterman, and there's no need to decide menu choices in advance. In the meantime, please let us know any dietary requirements with your RSVP.

  • What about gifts?

    We are so grateful to spend the day celebrating with you and we don't expect any gifts. However, should you wish to give a gift, a contribution to our honeymood fund would be greatly appreciated. There will be a box at the reception for cards.
