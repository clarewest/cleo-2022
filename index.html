The Bodleian Library

Ceremony Venue

The Bodleian is the University's main research library, established in 1602, and is made up several buildings including the Radcliffe Camera. Our ceremony will take place in Convocation House, a former University meeting room. The drinks reception will be held in Chancellor's Court, which was once the university's courtroom.

Gee's Restaurant & Bar

Reception Venue

Gee's is one of our favourite restaurants in Oxford. It is a grade II listed Victorian conservatory, originally built in 1897 to sell plants, flowers, fruit and vegetables.

How to Get Here

Below are some tips for getting here. Oxford is well-connected, but not very car-friendly. The reception venue is a 20 minute walk, or short taxi ride, from the ceremony venue. If you have any questions about getting here please feel free to get in touch with us.

Arriving by plane

London Heathrow and London Gatwick are the easiest airports for travelling to Oxford.

The airline bus service runs regularly between Oxford and Heathrow (1h30m) and Gatwick (2h30m), with stops in central Oxford. Return tickets cost £30 and £37, respectively. Tickets can be purchased online, on the app, or on the bus using cash or contactless payment.
