The Lighterman is a contemporary pub overlooking Regent's Canal. It is located in Granary Square, a large public square in our home borough of Camden, a short walk from Kings Cross and St Pancras Station.

How to Get Here

We recommend travelling by public transport or taxi. If you have any questions about getting here please feel free to get in touch with us.

  • By public transport
  • By car

Public Transport

The Orangery is a 15 minute walk from Holland Park (Central line) or High Street Kensington (Circle and District line). Bus numbers 41, 94, 148, 228, 295, 316 also stop nearby.

From Holland Park to the Lighterman, take the Central line (Eastbound) and Victoria line (Northbound) from Holland Park to King's Cross St Pancras, changing at Oxford Circus.

The Lighterman is a few minutes’ walk from King’s Cross and St Pancras stations. Follow signs for Regent’s Canal, cross the bridge over the canal and the Lighterman is on the right of Granary Square.

By Car

Pay and display parking is available at Abbotsbury Road Car Park. The charge is £3.70 per hour on Sundays from 07:30–18:30

The nearest car park to the Lighterman is the multi-storey Handyside Car Park which is open until 22:00. The evening rate (entry after 17:00) is £8.

The Neighbourhoods

Places to explore around the venues

Holland Park

A public park in Kensington, the former grounds of Cope Castle built in 1605

Kyoto Garden

A Japanese garden within Holland Park, gifted from the city of Kyoto in 1991

Coal Drops Yard

Formerly a derelict Victorian industrial yard, now a regenerated public space and shopping complex

Granary Square

Canal-side public leisure space in London's 'Knowledge Quarter', where we both work
